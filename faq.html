Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ECHO

General Questions

What is ECHO?

+

ECHO is a global music community platform that allows users to share playlists across different streaming services, discover new music through an interactive world map, and participate in live music streaming events.

Is ECHO free to use?

+

Yes, ECHO's basic features are free to use. However, you need to have an active subscription with supported streaming services (like Spotify or Apple Music) to play full tracks.

Which streaming platforms are supported?

+

ECHO currently supports:

  • Spotify
  • Apple Music
  • YouTube Music
  • Tidal

Account & Privacy

How do I create an account?

+

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button and choosing between a standard user account or an artist account. Artist accounts require verification of your artist status.

How is my data protected?

+

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and securely stored. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent. You can review our full privacy policy here.

Can I delete my account?

+

Yes, you can delete your account at any time through your account settings. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

Playlist Sharing

How do I share my playlists?

+

To share a playlist:

  1. Go to the "Share Playlist" section
  2. Copy your playlist link from your streaming service
  3. Fill in the playlist details (title, description, genre, etc.)
  4. Click "Share Playlist"

Can I transfer playlists between platforms?

+

Yes, ECHO allows you to convert playlists between supported streaming platforms. Note that some songs might not be available on all platforms due to licensing restrictions.

Livestreaming

How do I start a livestream?

+

To start a livestream:

  1. Go to the Livestream section
  2. Click "Schedule Stream"
  3. Fill in your stream details
  4. Use your preferred streaming software (OBS, Streamlabs, etc.)
  5. Connect using your unique stream key

What are the technical requirements for streaming?

+

Recommended requirements:

  • Internet upload speed: 5Mbps minimum
  • Supported streaming software (OBS, Streamlabs, etc.)
  • Audio interface (for high-quality sound)
  • Webcam (optional for video)

Technical Support

Which browsers are supported?

+

ECHO works best on recent versions of:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

I'm having technical issues. What should I do?

+

Try these steps:

  1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  2. Ensure your browser is up to date
  3. Check our status page for any known issues
  4. Contact support if the problem persists
